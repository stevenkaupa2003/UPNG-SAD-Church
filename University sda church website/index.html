About University SDA Church

We are located in the University of Papua New Guinea, Waigani Main Campus. Our purpose as a church is to be dynamic, exalting God in worship, proclaiming His Word, and upholding Adventist beliefs. Whether you are visiting for the first time or are a long time member, we hope that you find the information you are looking for. Use the Contact Us link on the Main Menu above if you have any questions or suggestions.
